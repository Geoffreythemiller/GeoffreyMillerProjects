Allentown Service Plaza

Tue 11/21/23 9:45-10:15 PM

There is no need to get on the turnpike. The employee parking lot is located on an access road off of Cetronia Rd. This road goes south off of Tilghman St. This is how the parents can pick up and drop off. If you look at Google Maps you can see a large parking lot behind the rest area building. We drop at the gate at the far end of the lot near the access gate.

Penn DOT Park & Ride parking lot

Wed 11/22/23, 12:25-12:55 AM

1102 Bill Smith Blvd, King Of Prussia, PA 19406. The location is near the SEPTA bus stop. It is between Home Depot and the parking lot of Lone Star Steakhouse & Saloon. Look at the map on Bus Stops page.

DON'T ENTER THE PENN DOT official address: 1102 Bill Smith Blvd. INTO YOUR GPS. It is likely that it will direct you to the wrong location. Almost all maps show the road south of the S. Gulph intersection as King of Prussia Rd instead of Bill Smith Blvd. Google Maps shows address 160 S Gulph Rd, King of Prussia, PA 19406.

Tickets

The cost of trip is: $115.00 (round); $65.00 (one-way). No additional fees are required.

Please reserve your ticket using the order form below and make your payment in the PayPal section below. There is no seat reservation without the payment.

Order Form

Please click on the button below and copy and paste its content into your email. Please enter your name into the subject area. Send the form to cpbus@cbtcorp.net.

In response to your email you will receive an email (generated immediately by the Autoresponder) that provides directions on how to read the OrderStatus.pdf file (updated daily) with your name on the list. Please notify us ASAP if your name was not placed on the list within 24 hrs from sending your order and making payment.

Please check the OrderStatus PDF a day after the payment and again right before the trip. If there is any error in the table, please notify us ASAP.

Please remember there is no seat reservation without the payment.

PayPal

Please follow these steps to make a payment with PayPal:

Do not click "Buy Now" unless you have entered your Cornell (or Ithaca College) email below on this page! If this information is not entered, your payment will be refunded.

Click “Buy Now” to be transferred to the PayPal secure website. Having a PayPal account is not required to purchase a ticket.

Notes

General

  1. You can change your bus stop location as needed without notifying us except if you want to use the Allentown stop from Philly to Cornell and you didn't select it in your Order Form.
  2. Two buses are scheduled for Thanksgiving trip if needed. The second bus will be ordered if we get more payments.
  3. It is sufficient to send an Order Form to put you on the wait list. We will notify you once a seat from a cancellation is available to you.
  4. When you order a seat on the waiting list no payment is required until we can offer you a seat following a cancellation from another student. This is to avoid a loss of PayPal fees in case of a refund.
  5. What to expect from the Waiting List can be found on our Bus Schedules page.

PayPal

  1. Refunds will only be granted up to one month before the trip is scheduled, unless there are buyers on the Waiting List that your spot can be transferred to.
  2. Make sure that the Credit Card owner is aware of the bus trip charge amount and its PAYPAL *FITNESSTRAC name on the statement if you are using your parent's card for this payment. (From time to time there is a parent filing a chargeback against the bus payment. It takes a lot of time on both sides to reverse it.)
  3. After paying, if you don't get a confirmation email from PayPal then please inform us by sending an email to cpbus@cbtcorp.net.
  4. Do not pay for the ride if there are no available seats. PayPal allows for refunds within 180 days from the payment date. Any refunds after that time will be paid immediately to your PayPal account. Please forward the refund confirmation email to cpbus@cbtcorp.net. PayPal refunds are issued ASAP. If you don’t get your refund within two days after the last day of the current round trip date then please contact us.
  5. PayPal no longer refunds a fee charged when the original transaction was made. As a result of this change we will refund your original payment minus fees charged by PayPal i.e. minus $.49 and minus 3.49% of the original sum paid.
